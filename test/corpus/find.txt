========
Find substring first implicit occurrence
========
FIND 'a' in str.
---
(source
    (find
        (substring_spec
            (literal_string))
        (identifier)))
========
Find substring first explicit occurrence
========
FIND FIRST OCCURRENCE OF 'a' in str.
---
(source
    (find
        (occurrence_spec)
        (substring_spec
            (literal_string))
        (identifier)))
========
Find substring all occurrences
========
FIND ALL OCCURRENCES OF 'a' in str.
---
(source
    (find
        (occurrence_spec)
        (substring_spec
            (literal_string))
        (identifier)))
========
Find section 
========
FIND ALL OCCURRENCES OF foo 
    IN SECTION OFFSET 12 LENGTH 5 OF str.
---
(source
    (find
        (occurrence_spec)
        (substring_spec
            value: (identifier))
        (section
            off: (number)
            len: (number))
        dobj: (identifier)))
